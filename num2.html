Mocha FlavoredCake

Ingredients

3 eggs, separated
1/3 cup of sugar
3 tablespoons of Fresh Milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup of all purpose flour
1 tablespoon of cocoa powder
1 teaspoon of instant coffee
3/4 teaspoon baking powder
3 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon of butter

Procedure

1.Sift flour with cocoa powder, instant coffee, and baking powder. Mix in the egg yolk mixture.
2. Separately whip egg whites with 2 tbsp of sugar until soft peaks. Fold in the egg yolk mixture.
3. Pour into a 6x2-inch cake pan lined with buttered parchment paper. Bake in a preheated 320°F oven for 20 – 30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean when inserted in the cake. Set aside to cool completely.
4. Remove cake from the pan and discard parchment paper. Cut into 3 equal parts and set aside.
5. Whip butter until light and mix in powdered sugar and cocoa powder. Add vanilla, dissolved coffee, and All Purpose Cream and continue to whip until soft peaks.
6. Assemble layers of cake and buttercream. Spread the side and top as well.
7. Make chocolate ganache by gently simmering All Purpose Cream. Pour chocolate chips and stir until smooth. Set aside to cool.
8. Pour ganache on cake and let it drip on the side. Top with preferred chocolate. Refrigerate until before serving.


You can flavor your icing with Mocha food flavor to match the cake.
